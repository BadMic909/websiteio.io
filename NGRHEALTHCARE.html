About NGR Healthcare

Founded on the belief that
health is everything

NGR Healthcare was built on a simple principle: accurate, timely diagnostic information changes lives. With ISO 9002 certification — a first for a multi-specialty centre in India — we have set the standard for diagnostic excellence.

Our Cardiology Centre alone performs over 500 angioplasties and 2,500 catheterisation procedures annually, with some of India's finest cardiologists on our panel.

  • ISO 9002 certified — India's first multi-specialty diagnostic centre
  • Sophisticated, state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment
  • Round-the-clock emergency services available
  • Insurance & TPA empanelled — cashless facility
  • Eco-friendly, sustainable healthcare practices
