Our Story

A Landmark of Whitefield's Dining Scene

Junction Restaurant has been a cornerstone of the Gunjur–Varthur community since 1998. Nestled near the Petrol Pump on Varthur Main Road, we've served generations of families, friends, and food lovers with warmth and consistency.

Our kitchen crafts bold North Indian curries, delicate Chinese noodles, and refined Continental plates — all under one welcoming roof. We believe every meal is an occasion, and every guest deserves to feel at home.
